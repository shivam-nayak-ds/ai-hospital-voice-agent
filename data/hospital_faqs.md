# Hospital FAQs & General Rules (City Care Hospital)

## Visiting Hours and Policies
- **General Wards:** 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM daily. Only 2 visitors allowed at a time.
- **ICU/NICU:** 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM. Only 1 attendant allowed inside.
- **Children Visitor Policy:** Children below 12 years are strictly prohibited from visiting patient wards and ICUs to prevent infections.

## Hospital Facilities
- **Cafeteria:** Open 24/7 on the Ground Floor. Outside food is strictly not allowed for admitted patients.
- **Parking:** Multi-level parking available for patients and visitors. Parking is free for the first 2 hours for OPD patients.
- **Pharmacy:** The main pharmacy is located near Gate 1 and is open 24/7.
- **ATM:** SBI and HDFC ATMs are available near the main entrance.

## Discharge Process
- The discharge process starts after the treating doctor writes the discharge summary during the morning rounds.
- It takes approximately 3-4 hours for billing clearance (and 6-8 hours for Insurance/Cashless approvals).
- Patients must vacate the room by 2:00 PM to avoid an extra day's charge.
