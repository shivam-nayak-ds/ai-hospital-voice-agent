# Laboratory and Diagnostics Guidelines (City Care Hospital)

## Fasting Requirements for Tests
Certain blood tests require strict fasting for accurate results. Fasting means ONLY water is allowed. No tea, coffee, milk, or food.
- **Lipid Profile:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory.
- **Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS):** 8-10 hours of fasting required.
- **Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH):** Fasting is preferred but not strictly mandatory.
- **Complete Blood Count (CBC):** No fasting required. Can be done anytime.

## Radiology and Imaging Rules
- **MRI Scans:** Patients with pacemakers, metal implants, or severe claustrophobia must inform the technician before the scan.
- **CT Scans:** If a contrast CT is ordered, the patient must provide a recent Kidney Function Test (KFT) report (not older than 7 days).
- **Ultrasound (USG) Whole Abdomen:** The patient must come with a full bladder. Drink 4-5 glasses of water one hour before the scan.

## Report Delivery
- **Routine Blood Tests:** Reports are available on the same day by 6:00 PM via WhatsApp and the Hospital Portal.
- **Culture Tests (Blood/Urine):** Takes 48-72 hours.
- **Biopsy/Histopathology:** Takes 5 to 7 working days.
- Hard copies can be collected from the Pathology Desk (Ground Floor) between 10 AM and 8 PM.
