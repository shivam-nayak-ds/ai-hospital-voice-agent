# City Care Multi-specialty Hospital - Master Knowledge Base

## 🏥 Hospital Overview
City Care Multi-specialty Hospital is a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing world-class medical services with a human touch.

### 📍 Location & Address
- **Address:** 34/6, New Palasia, near Janjeerwala Square, Indore, Madhya Pradesh - 452001.
- **Landmark:** Behind Apollo Tower.
- **City:** Indore.

### ⏰ Operating Hours
- **General OPD:** 10:00 AM to 08:00 PM (Monday to Saturday)
- **Emergency & Casualty:** 24/7 Open
- **Pharmacy:** 24/7 Open
- **ICU Visiting Hours:** 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 05:00 PM - 06:00 PM.

## 🚑 Emergency Services
- **Ambulance Helpline:** 102 (Toll-Free) or 0731-2445566.
- **Emergency Room (ER):** Located on the Ground Floor, Entrance Gate 2.
- **Trauma Care:** Specialized team available 24/7 for accidents and cardiac emergencies.

## 👨‍⚕️ Departments
1. **Cardiology:** Heart and Blood Pressure.
2. **Pediatrics:** Children's Health.
3. **Orthopedics:** Bones and Joints.
4. **Gynecology:** Women's Health.
5. **Neurology:** Brain and Nerves.
6. **Dermatology:** Skin Care.

## 📝 Visitor Policy
- Only one attendant is allowed with the patient in the ward.
- Children below 12 years are not allowed in the ICU area.
- Outside food is strictly prohibited for patients.

## 💳 Insurance & TPA
We support all major insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, and Government schemes like Ayushman Bharat.
