# Health Insurance & TPA Policies (City Care Hospital)

## Cashless Claim Process
1. **Pre-Authorization:** Cashless facility is available for all planned surgeries. Patients must submit the Pre-Authorization form at the TPA desk at least 48 hours before the surgery.
2. **Emergency Admissions:** For emergency cases, cashless approval must be initiated within 24 hours of admission.
3. **Required Documents:** 
   - Original Patient ID (Aadhar Card/Voter ID)
   - Health Insurance Card / E-card
   - Doctor's prescription advising admission
   - Previous medical reports

## Approved Insurance Providers
City Care Hospital is empaneled with the following major insurance companies for cashless treatment:
- **Star Health and Allied Insurance**
- **HDFC Ergo General Insurance**
- **ICICI Lombard**
- **Niva Bupa Health Insurance**
- **Care Health Insurance**
- **Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY):** Applicable only for General Ward admissions and selected government-approved surgeries.

## Important Rules
- Cashless facility is NOT available for OPD consultations and routine health checkups.
- If the insurance company denies the cashless claim, the patient must settle the entire bill in cash/card before discharge.
- The hospital is not responsible for partial claim approvals. The patient must pay the difference amount.
